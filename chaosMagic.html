1 v 1 Some effects work better for games with three or more players. Selecting "Duel" will filter out those effects.
The List has had several "collections" over the years. Pick one or define your own.
When a player would lose the game, they roll Vengeance. This might keep them in the game.
Some effects involve physical activity that may not be accessible for all players.
Minigames are more complicated effects that may pull attention away from the game. If you wish to play with them, you can set a round timer between games or choose to manually trigger one when you feel like it.
The Exemplar Chaos List involves a themed sub-list for some effects. Choose your theme table or include all of them.
Every effect belongs to one or more schools of magic. Choose which sorts of effects to include.
